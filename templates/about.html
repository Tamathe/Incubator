About the Incubator

A practical home for AI builders in medicine

Part community, part research pipeline, part project studio: the AI Incubator helps people at UK move from curiosity to useful work.

Mission

AI should be something students and clinicians can build with, not just hear about.

The AI Incubator exists to make artificial intelligence accessible, useful, and accountable in healthcare and medical education. Members learn by joining real projects, shaping new ideas, and working through the research steps that make AI work credible.

Purpose

Turn ideas into work

Weekly meetings create a regular rhythm for pitching, refining, staffing, and advancing projects.

Vision

Make AI capability local

Students and faculty across UK can build AI fluency by working on problems that matter here.

Values

Impact over novelty

The group emphasizes collaboration, accessibility, ethical research, and patient-centered outcomes.

How it works

A weekly project studio

Members can show up with any level of AI experience. The key is curiosity and willingness to build, write, test, or help organize the work.

01

Attend and listen

Hear project updates, learn the landscape, and see where your interests connect.

02

Join or pitch

Attach to an active project or bring your own idea for feedback and team formation.

03

Build toward evidence

Move through prototypes, IRBs, validation, presentations, manuscripts, and funding opportunities.
