FAQ

Common questions

The Incubator is designed to be welcoming without watering down the work.

Do I need AI experience?

No. Many members start with curiosity and learn through projects. There are roles for beginners and advanced builders.

Do I have to be in the College of Medicine?

No. The group welcomes collaborators from Medicine, Engineering, Public Health, Arts and Sciences, and other UK units.

Can I get authorship or research credit?

Active contributors can be included on presentations, publications, IRB protocols, and other scholarly outputs when their contribution supports it.

What if I only have a rough idea?

That is a good reason to come. The group can help turn a rough idea into a research question, prototype plan, or team pitch.
